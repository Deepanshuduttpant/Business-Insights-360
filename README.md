# 📊 Business Insights 360 Dashboard

End-to-end Power BI project with business insights across multiple domains.

---

## 🏠 Home Dashboard
![Home](images/Home.png)

📊 Overview dashboard showing key business KPIs like total revenue, profit, and growth trends.  
Helps stakeholders quickly understand overall business performance.

## 💰 Finance Dashboard
![Finance](images/Finance_view.png)

💰 Analyzed profit, revenue, and cost distribution across different segments.  
Identified high-cost areas and profit-driving categories for better financial decisions.

## 📈 Sales Dashboard
![Sales](images/Sales_view.png)

📈 Tracked sales performance across regions, products, and time.  
Highlighted top-selling products and seasonal trends to improve sales strategy.

## 📣 Marketing Dashboard
![Marketing](images/Marketing_view.png)

📊 Evaluated campaign performance and customer acquisition trends.  
Helped identify effective marketing channels and optimize budget allocation.

## 🚚 Supply Chain Dashboard
![Supply Chain](images/Supply_chain_view.png)

🚚 Monitored inventory levels, order fulfillment, and delivery performance.  
Detected bottlenecks and improved supply chain efficiency.

## 🧠 Problem Solved (Client Case)
![Problem Solved](images/Problem_solve_mr_haryali.png)

🧠 Solved a real-world business problem by analyzing Gross Margin (GM%) across customers and products.  
Identified top customers and products with high GM and those with low profitability.  
Provided actionable insights to focus on high-margin segments and improve low-performing areas.
